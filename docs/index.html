About

I am a university instructor, applied linguistics researcher, and software developer. I completed my Master of Science in Education at Temple University in 2016, and I am currently studying computer science with an emphasis on machine learning at the University of Colorado Boulder.

I am seeking part-time remote work or internship opportunities in data analytics or software development.
